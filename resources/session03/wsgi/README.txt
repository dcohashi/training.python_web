This calculator is implemented using the url to select the operator and arguments.
It supports addition, subtraction, multiplication, division.
Use the url, "http://localhost:8080/operator/arg1/arg2" to perform the
calculations, where operator is "add", "subtract", "multiply", or "divide",
and arg1 and arg2 are the two operands.  In the case of divide, the arg2
is the divisor.
